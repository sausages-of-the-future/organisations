River Thames licence

This additional licence permits you to fish at locks or weirs on the River Thames. They’re valid from 1 April to 31 March each year. You’re allowed to take another person fishing with your permit 3 times a year.

How much it costs

Licence type Price
Adults £28.50
Senior (over 60) concession £18.50
Disabled concession £18.50
Junior (under 16) concession £18.50
